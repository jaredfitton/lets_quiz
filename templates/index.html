1.) What is the the trig identity of d/dx f(cosx)

    cosx
    sec^2(x)
    -sinx
    n(x^(n-1))

2.) What is the the trig identity of d/dx f(sinx)

    cosx
    sec^2(x)
    -sinx
    n(x^(n-1))

3.) What is the the trig identity of d/dx f(x^n)

    cosx
    sec^2(x)
    -sinx
    n(x^(n-1))

4.) What is the the trig identity of d/dx f(tanx)

    cosx
    sec^2(x)
    -sinx
    n(x^(n-1))

The answer to question 1 is -sinx

The answer to question 2 is cosx

The answer to question 3 is n(x^(n-1))

The answer to question 4 is sec^2(x)
